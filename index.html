Bionic Functions

Both our Bionic Arm and Leg share the same technology, meaning it has up to 6 different grips. These are grouped together to make the transition between them very easy and mobile. To switch, amputees press a button on the back of their elbow. Prosthetists can also customise different grips and functions through an app on the phone.
